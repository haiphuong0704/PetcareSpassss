Section 1

Health Requirements

For the safety of all animals in our care, we require all pets to meet the following health standards before services are rendered:

  • Up-to-date vaccinations (Rabies, DHPP for dogs; FVRCP for cats)
  • Flea and tick treatment within the last 30 days
  • No contagious illnesses or open wounds
  • Pets showing signs of illness will be isolated and owners contacted
💉

Vaccination records may be requested at any visit. Please bring your pet's health booklet or have your vet send records in advance.

Section 2

Behavior & Aggression

We work with pets of all temperaments but reserve the right to stop a service if a pet poses a danger to staff or other animals. Please disclose any history of aggression at the time of booking.

  • Muzzling may be used for the safety of staff and your pet
  • Pets with extreme anxiety may be referred to a veterinarian before services
  • We do not use sedation — all calming methods are natural and gentle
Section 3

Emergency Procedures

In the event of a medical emergency, our staff is trained in pet first aid. We will contact you immediately and, if necessary, transport your pet to the nearest veterinary clinic.

Emergency Contacts

Please ensure your contact details are up to date at all times and provide an alternative emergency contact when booking overnight stays.
